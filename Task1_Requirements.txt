System Name: Inventory Management System
Target User: Shop owner or inventory manager of a small retail store

Main Goals:

Keep track of product stock levels

Automatically notify the user when stock is low

Record sales for reporting

Generate basic sales reports

Key Functionalities:

Add new products

View, update, or delete product details

Track stock quantity for each product

Set low-stock thresholds

Notify when stock is below threshold

Record sales with date and quantity

Generate 30-day sales reports
